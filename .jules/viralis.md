## 2024-05-23 - [VerifyContent] **Hypothesis:** When `navigator.share` is unavailable (desktop), users often just copy the link. By copying the *persuasive text* + URL automatically, we reduce the cognitive load of writing a post, increasing the likelihood of sharing context (who won, etc.). **Implementation:** Updated `verify-content.tsx` and `winner-ceremony.tsx` to append `shareUrl` to `shareText` in the clipboard fallback. **Outcome:** Improved context sharing on non-mobile devices.

## 2024-05-24 - [ViralLoop] **Hypothesis:** The previous share text "Congratulations to {name}" was passive. By changing it to "I just hosted a giveaway... Create yours free here:", we turn every share into an acquisition channel. **Implementation:** Updated translations in EN, ES, PT to include a direct CTA and loop mechanism. **Outcome:** Expected increase in "Create Giveaway" click-throughs from shared verification pages.

## 2024-05-25 - [WinnerCeremony] **Hypothesis:** Desktop users sharing via Twitter/WhatsApp buttons were sharing only text without the link, breaking the viral loop. By appending the URL to the share intents, we ensure every share carries the acquisition link. **Implementation:** Updated `shareTwitter`, `shareFacebook`, and `shareWhatsApp` in `winner-ceremony.tsx` to explicitly include `shareUrl`. **Outcome:** 100% of social shares now include a backlink to the app.
